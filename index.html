Marjorie Gross

I am a UX/UI Designer, with a strong background in inter-generational empathy, nonprofits, and creative problem solving.

My UX Skills

User Research

User Research is the base that everything else is built on and around. Having spent years working with people of all demographics, I am excited to talk to new people, ask questions, develop hypotheses, and conduct surveys. As the information is gathered, I work to sort it, and understand how it is going to direct my next steps.

Wireframing

Wireframing is a fun and important step in the design process, where I get to let my creativity loose, brainstorm wildly, and when a team brings wireframe ideas together, it is awesome to watch magic happen as different ideas bounce off of each other to form something new.

Iteration

Iteration, which happens non-linearly in my design process, is one of my favorite aspects of the process. The longer I spend with the User Research, the more I iterate wireframes, and the more a team communicates, the better the iterations become.
